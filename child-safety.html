Child Safety Standards Policy

Published by The CirkleUp Venture GmbH · May 26, 2026

CirkleUp maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward all content that exploits, endangers, or harms children and young people. This policy applies to every user, piece of content, and interaction on the platform without exception.

As a responsible company committed to creating a safe and positive community for all users, especially young people, CirkleUp fully supports and actively implements the Child Safety Standards Policy required by Google Play and the App Store. This policy is a critical step in ensuring that apps meet the highest standards for protecting children and young people.

Objectives

The following objectives are core to how CirkleUp is built and operated:

Reporting System

Users can report harmful or development-impairing content directly within the app in just a few clicks. All reports are reviewed promptly by our safety team.

Protection of Privacy

We respect the privacy of all users and ensure that no personal data is collected from children without the explicit consent of their parents or legal guardians.

Age Verification

Registration on CirkleUp is only possible for users aged 13 and above. Any subsequent change to a registered age requires verification by our support team, including submission of a scanned ID. If we become aware of incorrect age information, the account will be blocked immediately.

Users under 13 cannot access chat features, including sending and receiving chat requests or messages.

Content Security

Moderation System

Our moderation system, featuring a high number of moderators and fast response times, ensures that harmful or development-impairing content is removed promptly and does not remain publicly visible.

No Gambling or Gambling-Like Content

CirkleUp does not permit gambling, betting, loot boxes, or any gambling-like content on the platform.

Channel Search Restrictions

We intentionally restrict searches for channels on sensitive topics that could contain harmful content, to limit access for minors.

Blocking Channels for Minors

We maintain a list of channels containing potentially harmful content. Users who indicate they are under 13 during registration will not have access to these channels.

Images and Videos

Our AI-powered system reviews all images and videos before publication and blocks inappropriate content automatically. We are committed to removing content unsuitable for children and young people from our platform.

Grooming — Zero Tolerance

CirkleUp enforces a strict zero-tolerance policy toward all forms of cyber grooming. If any suspicion arises, we take immediate and comprehensive action:

  • Communication capabilities of the suspected account are restricted immediately
  • Our safety team conducts a thorough review of the reported behavior
  • If suspicion is confirmed, the account is permanently banned
  • The case may be referred to the relevant authorities

Advertising and Monetization

Advertising within CirkleUp is carefully curated to ensure it is age-appropriate and transparent. We do not permit misleading advertisements, inappropriate products or services, behavioral profiling ads, or undisclosed advertising.

Content Removal — AI and Moderation

The following types of content are handled instantly by AI-driven models, or within 24 hours by our moderation team if the models fail to catch them:

  • Illegal content (including CSAM, trafficking, terrorism) Removed
  • Pornographic and explicit sexual content Removed
  • Sexualized content involving minors, including sexual roleplay and sexualized depictions Removed
  • Graphic violence, real-world gore, and extreme harm including torture and extreme cruelty Removed
  • Hate speech, bullying, and harassment including racism, sexism, and homophobia Removed
  • Grooming, predatory, and exploitative contact Removed
  • Dangerous challenges and risky behavior including promoting dangerous stunts or encouraging drug usage Removed
  • Self-harm, suicide, and eating disorder content — removed and escalated to crisis support Removed
  • Mature content including vaping, alcohol, and dating — restricted to 18+ only channels

Our Commitments as Developer and Operator

As the developer and operator of CirkleUp, we are dedicated to:

  • Regularly reviewing the app's content and functionality to ensure compliance with child safety standards
  • Implementing mechanisms to report and address policy violations
  • Training our employees on the importance of protecting minors

The safety of children and young people is our top priority. We encourage all users to participate in fostering a respectful and secure environment.

Further Information

For more information on child safety standards and specific requirements, please visit the official Google Play Developer Policy page and App Store Review Guidelines.

Contact
