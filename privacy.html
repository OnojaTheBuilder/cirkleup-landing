4. Your Rights and Choices

Text Message Communications

CirkleUp may send transactional text messages (SMS), including one-time passcodes (OTP) for account verification and notifications related to membership applications, account status, and service updates, to users who have provided consent during registration.

During sign-up and login, users verify their phone number by entering a one-time passcode sent via SMS. By completing this verification and creating an account, users consent to receive automated transactional messages from CirkleUp. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on account activity.

You may opt out of non-essential SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. For assistance, contact info@cirkleup.com.

CirkleUp does not share phone numbers with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

  • Access and Updates: You may review and update your profile information through the App.
  • Communications Preferences: You may opt out of non-essential emails or push notifications in your account settings or by following unsubscribe instructions.
  • Account Deletion: You may request deletion of your account by contacting us at contact@cirkleup.com. Certain data may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
  • California Residents: If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA). CirkleUp does not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at info@cirkleup.com.
  • EEA and UK Residents: If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing and to data portability, subject to applicable law. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
